    • 1. Tech Rehab accepts the following types of payment: Cash, Cheque, Debit, E-transfer, PayPal, and most major Credit Cards.
    • 2. E-Transfers should be sent to support@techrehab.ca, we will follow up and confirm if your payment was received.
    • 3. Tech Rehab can also use the square terminal if a the client would like to pay in person, we are also able to manually process your credit/debit card over the phone.
